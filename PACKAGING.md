# Packaging Spec — Mother's Gold Spice (Jar v1)

Spec sheet to send to the Jabalpur, MP factory for a quote. One jar SKU across all pickle types at launch — only labels vary.

---

## 1. Jar specification

| Attribute | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| **Product** | Empty glass jar with wood-finish metal cap |
| **Capacity (nominal)** | **250g pickle** (≈ 250mL fill volume with 10% oil headspace) |
| **Brim volume** | ~280mL (gives headspace for oil + tamper seal) |
| **Shape** | Round, straight-sided (cylindrical body, no taper) |
| **Mouth diameter** | **63mm** (wide-mouth — standard for pickle, easier to scoop) |
| **Body diameter** | ~63–66mm |
| **Overall height (with cap)** | ~95–105mm — confirm with factory |
| **Glass color** | Clear flint glass (food-grade, lead-free) |
| **Glass weight** | ~150–180g per empty jar (sturdy feel, ships well) |
| **Finish** | Glossy, no defects, no bubbles, no seam lines visible on body |
| **Use case stated** | Acidic and oily food (pickle in mustard oil) — material must be tested for this |

---

## 2. Cap specification

| Attribute | Spec |
|-----------|------|
| **Material** | Tinplate or aluminum (food-grade) |
| **Finish (outer)** | **Wood-look heat-transfer print** (matte oak or walnut tone preferred) |
| **Inner coating** | **Food-grade lacquer rated for ACIDIC + OILY contents** (this is critical — uncoated metal will corrode and contaminate pickle) |
| **Closure type** | Twist-on (continuous thread) preferred over press-on lug cap |
| **Liner / gasket** | PVC-free, food-grade liner that holds an airtight seal |
| **Tamper evidence** | Either built-in tamper band on cap, OR confirm we'll add a separate PVC/PET shrink seal over the cap (we'll buy these separately if not part of cap) |
| **Diameter** | Matches 63mm jar mouth — confirm thread spec (likely 63/2030 or 63/400) |

**Critical confirmation for the factory:** the wood-finish print must be food-safe and survive normal handling (no flaking after rubbing, no bleeding when wiped with damp cloth). Ask for a sample before placing bulk order.

---

## 3. Label area (informational — we print labels separately)

Once jar dimensions are confirmed, we'll design the label to match. For the design brief, we'll need from the factory:

- **Label panel height** (jar body height between any shoulder curves and any base bevel)
- **Label panel circumference** (= body diameter × π)
- Whether the jar has any embossing / mold marks on the body (we want the label face **smooth and clean**)

## 6. Compliance & paperwork required

The factory must provide / confirm:

- ✅ **Food-grade material certification** — IS 14536 or equivalent BIS standard for glass containers used with food
- ✅ **Food-grade lacquer certification** for cap inner coating (specifically for acidic + oily contents)
- ✅ **GST invoice** (we will provide our GST number once registered)
- ✅ **Lab report** for jar and cap (if available — not always provided, but ask)
- ✅ **No lead, no cadmium** in glass or printed surfaces

We will provide them:
- ✅ Our **FSSAI license number** (once received)
- ✅ Our **GST registration number** (once received)
- ✅ Final purchase order with quantity + delivery address + ship-by date

## 7. Open questions for the factory

Send these in your first email along with the spec:

1. Do you stock a standard round 63mm wide-mouth 250g pickle jar, or is this a custom order? (Custom = extra cost + lead time)
2. What is your **minimum order quantity** for round wide-mouth 250g jars?
3. Can you supply **caps with wood-finish print** in-house, or do you source caps from a separate cap manufacturer?
4. What is the lead time from PO to factory dispatch?
5. Do you offer **printed branding directly on the cap** (e.g., a small "MGS" logo)? If yes, what's the MOQ and added cost per cap?
6. Do you have **sample jars + caps** we can buy in small quantity (5–10 units) to test for fit, finish, and the wood-print quality before committing to a bulk order? Most factories will charge a small fee + ship them.

## 8. Decision log

| Decision | Choice | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| Capacity at launch | 250g | Premium artisan positioning; lower price point for trial purchase |
| Shape | Round straight-sided | Lower MOQ, lower cost, faster to market vs square. Square reserved for v2 relaunch. |
| Lid | Metal cap with wood-finish print | Looks premium without the cost/leakage issues of real wood |
| Branding on jar | Label only at launch | Same jar across all pickle types; label varies. Lowest complexity. |
| Mouth | Wide (63mm) | Easier to scoop, retail standard for pickle |
| Material | Clear flint glass | Showcases the product, standard, cheapest |

---

## 9. Next steps after factory replies

1. Compare quotes from at least one other supplier (try IndiaMART search: *"250g round glass pickle jar manufacturer"*) so you have a price baseline.
2. Order **sample units** (5–10 each) before placing the bulk order. Test:
   - Cap holds an airtight seal after 1 week with oil + pickle inside
   - Wood-finish doesn't peel under normal handling
   - Jar survives a 1-meter drop test onto wood (acceptable: cap dents, body unbroken)
   - Label adheres cleanly to the body panel
3. Use the sample to **shoot real product photos** for the website and Amazon listing.
4. Get bulk **label printing quote** (separate vendor — local Bangalore print shop). Spec the label panel dimensions from §3.
5. Place pilot order (Tier A — 500 jars) once samples pass.
